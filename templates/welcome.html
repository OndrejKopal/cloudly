What is F*ckmecloudly?

F*ckmecloudly is a tool for centralized management of the cloud (both public and private).
It helps you take control of both private and public clouds and put it into a central place. F*ckmecloudly is open source and completely free. You can find support on the mailing list and on IRC, or get professional support.
